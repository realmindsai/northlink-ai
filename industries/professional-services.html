How each one works

Real pain points, real solutions, real time savings. Three of these have live demos you can try right now.

📄 Document Processing

Try Live Demo →

🧾 Tax Return Prep

"Tax returns start with a pile of blurry receipts. Someone spends half a day sorting, categorising, and entering data. Then they find a duplicate."
AI extracts transactions from receipts, bank statements, and PAYG summaries, auto-categorises expenses, detects duplicates, flags depreciation items, and estimates the refund. Your accountant reviews the output instead of doing the data entry.
Half a day of data entry → 2 minutes
Try Live Demo →

📜 Contract Review

"Contract review takes hours per document. A 60-page supply agreement lands on your desk. You read every clause, checking against your firm's playbook. Most are standard. But the three that aren't could cost your client millions."
AI highlights clauses by risk level (green/amber/red), compares against your firm's preferred wording, generates a risk heatmap, and produces a tracked-changes redline. You focus on the clauses that matter.
Hours per document → 45 seconds
Try Live Demo →

📂 Evidence Chronology

"Building a chronology from 50 documents takes days. Emails, contracts, invoices, meeting notes, and text messages from multiple parties spanning 18 months. A paralegal manually assembles the timeline."
AI builds a dated timeline from the document pile, links each event to source documents, and identifies gaps in the evidence chain. What took days of paralegal time happens in seconds.
Days of paralegal time → 30 seconds

👤 Client & Matter Management

👤 Client Intake Automation

"New client onboarding takes 2 hours of form-filling. Manual data entry from identification documents, conflict checks, engagement letters. Every new matter starts with the same administrative drag."
AI extracts client details from ID documents, runs conflict checks against your existing client base, drafts engagement letters, and populates the practice management system. Onboarding becomes a review task, not a data entry task.
2 hours → 15 minutes

📄 Matter Summarisation

"Catching up on a file that's been dormant for 6 months takes half a day. You re-read correspondence, notes, and file history just to remember where things stand."
AI generates a current-state summary of any matter: key dates, outstanding actions, last communications, and upcoming deadlines. Pick up any file in minutes instead of hours.
Half a day → 5 minutes

⏱️ Time Entry & Billing

"Reconstructing timesheets at month-end is guesswork. Under-recording of billable time due to manual entry lag costs the firm thousands every month."
AI monitors work activity — documents opened, emails sent, meetings attended — and suggests time entries with narrative descriptions in real time. No more end-of-month reconstruction.
Month-end guesswork → Real-time capture

📋 Compliance & Risk

📣 Regulatory Update Monitoring

"The new privacy amendment passed and we missed the 30-day compliance window. Manual monitoring of legislation, case law, and regulatory changes just doesn't scale."
AI monitors relevant regulatory feeds, flags changes affecting your practice areas and clients, and suggests required actions. You hear about it the same day it happens, not after the deadline passes.
Missed compliance windows → Same-day alerts

🕵️ AML/KYC Screening

"CDD checks take 45 minutes per new client. Manual identity verification, PEP screening, and sanctions checking. Multiply that by every new matter and it's a serious overhead."
AI automates customer due diligence: verifies identity documents, screens against PEP and sanctions lists, and generates risk assessments. Compliance is thorough and fast.
45 minutes per client → 5 minutes

💲 Trust Account Reconciliation

"Monthly trust reconciliation takes a full day and everyone hates it. Manual matching of trust receipts, disbursements, and ledger entries. One mistake and the regulator comes knocking."
AI reconciles trust transactions automatically, flags discrepancies, and generates compliance-ready reports. What used to ruin someone's day now takes half an hour.
Full day → 30 minutes

📈 Firm Growth & Efficiency

📝 Proposal & Pitch Generation

"Writing a pitch for a new client takes 2 days. Manual assembly of credentials, case studies, and fee proposals. Half the time you're reformatting old documents."
AI generates customised proposals from your firm's credentials, relevant experience, and standard fee structures, tailored to the prospect's industry and needs. You refine instead of build from scratch.
2 days → 2 hours

🔍 Knowledge Management

"I know we advised on this exact issue 2 years ago but can't find the memo. Institutional knowledge locked in email threads and file servers, invisible to anyone who wasn't on the original matter."
AI indexes all firm documents and communications, enabling natural-language search across matters, memos, and precedents. Ask a question and get the answer with source links in seconds.
Can't find it → Instant retrieval

🎓 Training Material Generation

"Creating CPD content takes senior partners away from billable work. Manual preparation of internal training materials and CPD presentations eats into revenue-generating time."
AI generates training materials from firm precedents, recent case law, and regulatory updates, structured for CPD compliance. A senior partner reviews a draft instead of writing from blank.
Days of senior time → Draft in 1 hour

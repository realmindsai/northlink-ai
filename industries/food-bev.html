How each one works

Real pain points, real solutions, real time savings. Three of these have live demos you can try right now.

📋 Compliance & Labelling

📋 Label Compliance Checker

"FSANZ Standard 1.2.1 has 47 mandatory elements. Our compliance manager checks them by eye. Mistakes still get through — and a single missed allergen declaration can trigger a recall."
AI scans food labels against the full FSANZ standards library, flags violations with specific clause references, checks allergen declarations, nutritional panel accuracy, and mandatory warning statements. Returns a compliance report in 30 seconds.
2 hours manual review → 30 seconds
Try Live Demo →

📰 Regulatory Change Monitoring

"A new FSANZ standard dropped and we found out from a customer complaint. We had no idea the rules had changed."
AI monitors FSANZ, state food authorities, and export-market regulatory bodies for updates. When a change is published, it cross-references your product catalogue and flags exactly which products are affected and what needs to change.
Customer complaints → Real-time alerts

⚠️ Allergen Cross-Reference

"We reformulated 3 products but forgot to update the allergen matrix. The mismatch sat there for weeks."
AI cross-references ingredient specifications against product labels and allergen declarations. When an ingredient changes, it flags every downstream product that needs a label update — before the mismatch reaches the shelf.
Manual spreadsheet tracking → Automated consistency checks

🔍 Traceability & Recall

🔍 Batch Traceability

"Tracing a batch takes days of spreadsheet cross-referencing. Three systems, no links between them, and someone always has the file open."
AI maps the full genealogy tree: suppliers → raw materials → production runs → finished goods → distribution → retail. Type a batch number and see the complete forward and backward trace in 3 seconds.
Days of cross-referencing → 3 seconds
Try Live Demo →

🚨 Recall War Room

"A recall notice at 2am means all-hands chaos. Which batches? Which SKUs? Which customers? Everyone scrambles through paper records and shared drives."
AI traces contamination forward through the entire supply chain — from the affected ingredient to every batch, SKU, and customer that received it. Scopes the recall in 2.8 seconds and drafts communications for customers, regulators, and internal teams.
All-hands chaos → 2.8 seconds to scope
Try Live Demo →

📄 Supplier Audit Trail

"Our supplier changed their soy source and didn't tell us. We only found out when a customer flagged an allergen issue."
AI continuously monitors supplier certifications, Certificates of Analysis, and specification documents. When an ingredient source shifts or a certification lapses, you get an alert — not a customer complaint.
No upstream visibility → Continuous monitoring

⚖️ Production & Quality

⚖️ Recipe Scaling & Costing

"Scaling from 50kg to 500kg batch isn't just multiply by 10. Emulsifiers behave differently, yield losses change, and equipment has its own limits."
AI adjusts recipes for batch size, accounting for non-linear ingredient behaviour, yield losses, ingredient interactions, and equipment constraints. Produces accurate scaled costings in minutes instead of trial-and-error.
Trial-and-error scaling → Accurate costings in minutes

📅 Shelf Life Prediction

"We set shelf life conservatively and waste 12% of production. But accelerated testing for every formulation tweak costs a fortune."
AI predicts shelf life from formulation, packaging, and storage conditions using historical stability data and degradation models. Optimise shelf life without expensive testing for every change.
12% production waste → Reduce waste 30%

📈 Production Line OEE

"We know the line stops but not why or how often. The whiteboard tracking system gave up months ago."
AI monitors production line performance in real time, categorises every stop event, and identifies the patterns driving availability, performance, and quality losses. Turns invisible downtime into actionable data.
Invisible downtime → Real-time OEE dashboard

💰 Supply Chain & Demand

📈 Demand Forecasting

"Christmas orders caught us off guard again. We ordered based on last year's numbers plus gut feel, and still ran out of stock."
AI analyses historical sales, seasonal patterns, promotions, and external signals — weather, events, market trends — to forecast demand 8 weeks out. Turns reactive ordering into proactive planning.
Gut feel ordering → 8-week demand forecast

📦 Inventory Optimisation

"We threw out $40k of expired ingredients last quarter. FIFO tracking on spreadsheets just doesn't work at scale."
AI optimises stock levels, rotation schedules, and reorder points to minimise waste while preventing stockouts. Tracks expiry dates across all ingredients and flags items approaching end-of-life before they become write-offs.
$40k expired ingredients → Reduce spoilage 35%

💰 Supplier Price Benchmarking

"Are we getting a good price on our flour? No idea. We've used the same supplier for years and never compared."
AI benchmarks your purchase prices against market rates and alternative suppliers for commodity ingredients. Flags savings opportunities and tracks price trends so you negotiate from data, not guesswork.
No price visibility → Save 8-12% on key inputs

How each one works

Real pain points, real solutions, real time savings. Three of these have live demos you can try right now.

📋 Documentation & Compliance

Try Live Demo →

📄 POD Reconciliation

"Drivers bring back crumpled PODs with illegible signatures. Someone has to match each one to an invoice. One wrong digit means a payment dispute."
AI extracts consignment numbers, receivers, signatures, and GPS coords from messy POD photos, then matches them to invoices automatically. Flags mismatches and missing dockets before they become disputes.
4 hours → 30 seconds

📋 Chain of Responsibility Documentation

"CoR compliance means drowning in driver logbooks, maintenance records, and rest break tracking across the whole fleet."
AI monitors driver hours, rest breaks, and vehicle maintenance records for Chain of Responsibility compliance. Auto-generates audit documentation and flags breaches before an inspector does.
Manual tracking → Automated compliance
Try Live Demo →

💰 Carrier Invoice Reconciliation

"Every month, someone manually checks fuel surcharges, wait times, and zone rates across dozens of carrier invoices against the contract. Overcharges slip through."
AI extracts line items from carrier invoices (even scanned), matches against contract rates, highlights overcharges, and drafts dispute emails ready to send.
3 hours → 45 seconds

🗺️ Fleet & Routing

Try Live Demo →

🗺️ Route Optimisation

"48 deliveries across 4 routes. Driver 2 calls in at 6am. Someone spends an hour redistributing stops by hand. Time windows get missed."
AI reoptimises routes across remaining drivers, considering delivery windows, vehicle capacity, and traffic. The whole day is replanned in seconds, not hours.
1 hour manual rework → 2 seconds

🚗 Fleet Lifecycle Management

"We replace trucks when they break down, not when we should. Last year a blown engine cost us $45k and two weeks off the road."
AI analyses maintenance costs, fuel efficiency, and utilisation trends per vehicle to recommend optimal replacement timing. Extends fleet life while avoiding catastrophic failures.
Reactive replacement → Extend fleet life 15%

⛽️ Fuel Efficiency Monitoring

"Fuel is our biggest cost and we can't tell who's wasting it. Some drivers use 30% more fuel on the same route."
AI monitors fuel consumption per driver, route, and vehicle, identifying inefficient driving patterns — harsh braking, excessive idling, speeding — and suggests corrections with driver-level reporting.
No visibility → Reduce fuel spend 8-12%

📦 Warehouse & Operations

🚶 Pick-Path Optimisation

"Pickers walk 12km per shift and still miss items. The pick list is in entry order, not walking order."
AI sequences pick lists by optimal walking path through warehouse zones, reducing travel distance and errors. Pickers follow a logical route instead of zigzagging across the floor.
12km per shift → Reduce pick time 25%

📦 Demand-Driven Slotting

"Our best sellers are at the back of the warehouse because that's where they were put when we first stocked them."
AI analyses order frequency and velocity to recommend optimal warehouse slot positions. Fast movers get placed closest to dispatch, seasonal items rotate automatically.
Static layout → Cut pick time 20%

🚢 Dock Scheduling

"Three trucks arrive at once and two sit idle for 2 hours. No one coordinates inbound deliveries with dock availability."
AI schedules dock appointments based on carrier ETAs, unload times, and available bays. Trucks arrive at staggered times and dock space is used efficiently.
2-hour wait times → Eliminate idle trucks

📡 Customer & Visibility

📡 Delivery Prediction & Exception Handling

"Customers call asking where their delivery is. Our dispatcher becomes a full-time phone operator instead of managing the fleet."
AI predicts delivery ETAs in real time, proactively notifies customers of delays, and suggests alternatives for exceptions. Customers know before they need to call.
Reactive phone calls → Proactive ETA updates

💬 Customer Communication Automation

"Our dispatcher spends half the day on the phone updating customers on delivery status. Three hours a day, just saying 'it's on its way'."
AI auto-sends status updates at key milestones — dispatched, en route, 30 minutes away, delivered — via SMS and email. Customers stay informed without a single phone call.
3 hours/day on phone → Save 3 hours/day

📈 Loss & Damage Analytics

"We know we're losing money on damage claims but not where or why. Same products keep arriving broken and nobody connects the dots."
AI analyses damage claims by route, driver, product type, and packaging to identify root causes and recommend preventive actions. Turns scattered incident reports into actionable patterns.
No pattern analysis → Reduce claims 30%

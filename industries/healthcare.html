How each one works

Real pain points, real solutions, real time savings. Three of these have live demos you can try right now.

🎤 Clinical Documentation

🎤 Clinical Note Generator

"A 15-minute consultation means 20 minutes of admin. 40% of practitioner time goes to paperwork instead of patients."
AI listens to the consultation and generates a structured SOAP note in real time. Highlights clinical terms with ICD-10 and MBS codes. Filters small talk automatically so only clinically relevant content makes it into the record.
20 min per note → 0 seconds
Try Live Demo →

✉️ Referral Letter Drafting

"Writing referral letters eats 30 minutes per patient. The specialist needs history, medications, and specific questions — all pulled from different screens."
AI generates referral letters from clinical notes, including relevant patient history, current medications, and specific referral questions tailored to the specialist's area. You review and send instead of drafting from scratch.
30 minutes → 2 minutes

📋 Patient Intake Automation

"New patient forms take 15 minutes to process into the system. Reception is typing demographics while patients are waiting."
AI extracts patient demographics, medical history, current medications, and allergies from paper or PDF intake forms and populates the practice management system automatically. Flags inconsistencies for human review.
15 minutes → 30 seconds

📅 Scheduling & Patient Flow

📅 No-Show Predictor

"No-shows cost us $1,200 a day. That's 4 empty slots. Phone confirmations don't reach the high-risk patients."
AI scores each appointment by no-show risk using patient history, demographics, and behavioural patterns. High-risk bookings get targeted interventions — SMS reminders, pre-payment prompts, or overbooking recommendations. Cuts no-shows from 15% to 8%.
15% no-show rate → 8% with AI intervention
Try Live Demo →

🕒 Appointment Scheduling Optimisation

"Tuesdays are chaos, Thursdays are empty. We can't figure out why, and patients keep leaving because they can't get a slot."
AI analyses appointment patterns and patient preferences to suggest optimal scheduling. Fills gaps, smooths peaks, and ensures practitioners are evenly utilised across the week.
Feast-or-famine scheduling → 90%+ utilisation

🔔 Waitlist Management

"Patients wait 3 weeks for a cancellation spot we filled 2 days later by calling around manually."
AI instantly matches cancellations to waitlisted patients by urgency, preference, and availability. Sends automated offers via SMS with one-tap confirmation. Fills the slot in minutes, not days.
Days to fill cancellation → Filled in minutes

💳 Billing & Compliance

💳 MBS Coding Assistant

"Billing codes are a guessing game. Level B or Level C? Did we hit the 20-minute threshold? Over-code and risk an audit. Under-code and lose revenue."
AI analyses clinical notes and recommends MBS item numbers with confidence scores. Flags audit risks — consultation duration too short for the claimed level, missing documentation for complex items. Maps evidence from the clinical record to each code.
Guesswork → 94% coding accuracy
Try Live Demo →

📚 Compliance Document Drafting

"AHPRA audit prep means two weeks of scrambling — hunting for CPD records, supervision logs, and policy documents across filing cabinets and shared drives."
AI maintains a continuously updated compliance portfolio. Auto-generates audit-ready documentation from practice records, tracks CPD hours, monitors policy expiry dates, and assembles everything into the format the auditor expects.
2 weeks of scrambling → 2 days, audit-ready

💰 Bulk Billing Optimisation

"We bulk bill but lose money on complex consults. No idea which patient types and consult types are actually profitable to bulk bill."
AI analyses consult complexity, time spent, and billing patterns to recommend an optimal billing strategy per patient type. Identifies which patients should be privately billed and where mixed billing recaptures lost revenue.
Invisible revenue leakage → $40k/year recovered

📈 Practice Growth

💬 Patient Communication Engine

"Recall reminders go out late or not at all. Patients fall through the cracks and we lose them to the clinic down the road."
AI automates recall reminders, follow-up scheduling, and health screening prompts based on patient history and clinical guidelines. Sends the right message at the right time via the patient's preferred channel.
Ad-hoc reminders → 95% recall compliance

⭐ Online Reputation Management

"We have 3 stars on Google and don't know why. One bad review sits there for months with no response."
AI monitors reviews across platforms, drafts professional responses, identifies common complaints from sentiment analysis, and prompts satisfied patients for reviews at the right moment — after a positive consultation, not during billing.
3 stars, unmanaged → 4.5 stars in 90 days

🎓 Staff Training & Onboarding

"New practitioners take 3 months to learn our systems, our protocols, and how we do things here. We don't have a structured onboarding program."
AI creates personalised onboarding pathways from practice protocols and clinical guidelines. Generates training materials, quizzes on practice-specific workflows, and tracks competency progression so new staff get up to speed faster.
3 months onboarding → 3 weeks

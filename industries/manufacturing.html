📄 Quote from Drawings

"The estimator opens the drawing, counts features, checks material prices, calculates setup time, types it all into a spreadsheet. Every. Single. Job. Three hours gone."

📧 RFQ-to-Quote Copilot

"A 12-page RFQ arrives with specs, drawings, and compliance requirements buried in email attachments. Someone has to read it all before we can even start quoting. Tender packs sit in the inbox for days."
AI parses email and PDF tender packs, extracts structured requirements, identifies key specifications, and builds a draft quote with source attribution linking every line item back to the original document.
Days in the inbox → 2 minutes

📈 Historical Job Costing

"Every new quote starts from zero, even though we did a similar job 6 months ago. Nobody remembers what we charged or what it actually cost."
AI matches new RFQs against your past job costings — adjusting for material price changes, scope differences, and lessons learned. Gives you a data-backed starting estimate instead of guesswork.
Starting from zero → Save 2 hours per quote

⚙️ Production & Operations

Try Live Demo →

📅 Scheduling Optimiser

"When the best customer calls with an urgent order, someone spends 45 minutes rearranging the whiteboard. And still misses a deadline. A rush job throws the whole week off."
AI optimises work order scheduling across machines, minimising changeover time and meeting deadlines. Drag a rush job onto the Gantt chart and watch AI re-optimise the entire week in seconds.
45-min manual rearrangement → 2 seconds

🔧 Predictive Maintenance

"Machines fail without warning, halting production for hours or days. Unplanned shutdowns cost $100k in lost output and emergency repairs."
Sensor-driven AI monitors vibration, temperature, and performance patterns to predict equipment failures before they happen. Schedules maintenance during planned downtime, not during a critical production run.
$100k unplanned shutdowns → $100k savings on $25k investment

🔄 Production Planning & Changeover

"We waste 2 hours per day on changeovers because jobs are sequenced based on when orders came in, not what makes sense for the machines."
AI sequences jobs to minimise changeover between similar setups — grouping materials, tooling, and machine configurations. Reduces unnecessary tool changes and keeps machines running.
2 hours per day on changeovers → Cut changeover time 40%

✅ Quality & Compliance

📷 Computer Vision QC

"Defects slip through to customers because human inspection misses subtle surface flaws at production speed. One bad batch and we lose the account."
AI camera systems detect surface defects, dimensional errors, and assembly mistakes in real time on the production line. Flags issues before parts leave the factory, not after a customer complaint.
Human inspection misses → 99.2% detection rate

📈 Statistical Process Control

"We only find drift after a bad batch. Manual checks every 50 units miss gradual process drift until it's too late."
AI monitors production parameters continuously — temperature, pressure, dimensions, cycle times — and alerts operators when tolerance bands are trending toward limits, not after they've been breached.
Find drift after bad batch → Catch drift in real time

📋 Compliance Documentation

"Audit prep takes a week every quarter. Someone manually assembles quality records, certifications, and test results from three different systems."
AI auto-generates audit-ready documentation from production data with full traceability — linking test results to batch numbers, certifications to suppliers, and quality records to specific jobs.
1 week per quarter → 1 day

📦 Supply Chain

🚨 Supplier Risk Monitoring

"We didn't know our supplier was struggling until delivery failed. No visibility into their financial health or capacity until it's too late."
AI monitors supplier signals — news, financials, delivery patterns, industry chatter — and flags risks before they hit your production line. Gives you time to find alternatives, not scramble for them.
Blindsided by failures → Early warning system

📦 Demand-Supply Matching

"We're always either overstocked or scrambling. Manual forecasting from gut feel and spreadsheets means we carry too much of what we don't need and run out of what we do."
AI matches demand signals to supplier lead times, optimising order quantities and timing. Reduces excess inventory while ensuring critical materials arrive when needed.
Overstocked or scrambling → Reduce inventory carrying 25%

💰 Material Price Tracking

"Steel prices moved 15% and we didn't adjust quotes for 3 weeks. Manual price checking across multiple suppliers means margins erode before anyone notices."
AI tracks commodity and material prices across suppliers in real time. Alerts when margins are at risk on open quotes, suggests price adjustments, and flags the best time to lock in supply contracts.
3-week price lag → Real-time margin protection

How each one works

Real pain points, real solutions, real time savings. Three of these have live demos you can try right now.

📈 Estimating & Tendering

📐 Takeoffs from Plans

"The estimator prints the plan, counts windows, measures walls. A house takes a day. A development takes a week."
AI reads architectural plans and traces walls, counts openings, shades wet areas, and calculates material quantities. Outputs a line-item takeoff with framing, windows, doors, waterproofing, and plasterboard.
1 full day → 30 seconds
Try Live Demo →

📈 Historical Cost Matching

"Every quote starts from scratch, even though we did a similar job 6 months ago."
AI matches new project specs against your past job costings — adjusting for material price changes, location, and scope differences. Gives you a starting estimate in minutes, not hours.
3 hours per quote → 15 minutes

📩 Tender Response Drafting

"A 40-page tender spec means 2 days just writing the response, before we even price the job."
AI reads the tender specification, extracts all requirements, and drafts a compliant response document. You review and refine instead of writing from blank.
2 days → 2 hours

🏗️ Site & Project Management

📸 Photo to Progress Report

"Every Friday: walk the job, take 30 photos, spend 2 hours writing a report from memory."
AI analyses site photos, identifies trade progress (framing 85%, electrical started, plumbing stalled), compares to last week, and generates a formatted report with risk flags and weather notes.
2 hours → 30 seconds
Try Live Demo →

📅 Scheduling & Dispatch

"One tradie calls in sick and the whole week's schedule falls apart across 3 sites."
AI optimises crew allocation across your active projects — factoring in skills, travel time, dependencies, and weather. Rebalances the schedule when things change.
45 min reworking → Instant rebalance

🛠️ Subcontractor Tracking

"We keep using the same subbies because we don't have time to evaluate alternatives."
AI builds a scorecard for each subcontractor — on-time rate, defect history, compliance documentation, pricing trends. Recommends alternatives when performance dips.
Gut feel → Data-driven decisions

🌧️ Weather Impact Planning

"Rain forecast on Thursday means we scramble to rearrange the concrete pour."
AI monitors BOM forecasts and cross-references your schedule. Flags weather-sensitive tasks 5 days out and suggests rescheduling options before the crew gets sent home.
Reactive scramble → 5-day advance warning

📋 Compliance & Safety

📋 SWMS Generation

"Writing a new SWMS for every job takes half a day, and they all look the same anyway."
AI generates Safe Work Method Statements from the job scope, site conditions, and trade activities. Pre-populates hazards, controls, and PPE requirements. You review and sign off.
4 hours → 5 minutes

🚨 Incident Pattern Detection

"We log near-misses but nobody reads the spreadsheet until after an actual injury."
AI analyses incident and near-miss reports across all your sites. Spots patterns — same task, same time of day, same subcontractor — and alerts you before a trend becomes a claim.
Invisible patterns → Early warning system

✅ BIM Clash Detection

"The plumber and the sparky clashed in the ceiling space — again. $8,000 rework."
AI scans BIM models and flags structural, mechanical, and electrical clashes before construction starts. Saves rework costs and schedule delays.
$8k rework per clash → Caught before pour

💰 Finance & Claims

📂 Variation Claim Builder

"The client asked for extra power to the shed. It was in an email. Or a text. Or a site diary. Building the claim means hunting through a year of correspondence."
AI assembles emails, texts, photos, diary entries, and invoices into a chronological variation claim. Links each event to its source document. Calculates the cost build-up and flags gaps in evidence.
4 hours → 2 minutes
Try Live Demo →

💰 Invoice & Payment Chasing

"We've got $180k outstanding and nobody has time to chase it."
AI monitors your aged receivables, auto-drafts follow-up emails at 30/60/90 days, predicts which clients will pay slow based on history, and flags accounts heading for trouble.
$180k outstanding → 40% reduction in aged debt
